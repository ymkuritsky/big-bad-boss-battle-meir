# Big Bad Boss Battle 4

Current version:
- Big Bad Boss Battle 4 has 30 levels.
- It has 30 levels across regular fight worlds and special mission worlds.
- Every level has a timer. In each two-level pair, the first level has 2 minutes and the second level has 3 minutes.
- The place order is Florida, New York, Antarctica, Paris, Phoenix, Russia, China, Canada, Africa Savanna, Washington D.C., Iran Palace, Israel, and Egyptian Palace.
- Levels 1 and 2 take place on a Florida beach with palm trees, ocean water, sand, and seashells.
- Levels 3 and 4 take place inside a giant Statue of Liberty maze in New York. The player is a face, the boss chases them, and reaching EXIT at the head beats the level.
- Levels 5 and 6 take place in Antarctica as a Penguin Treasure hunt. The full superhero runs across three icy screens while the boss and boss powers chase them.
- Levels 7 and 8 take place in Paris by the Eiffel Tower as regular Big Bad Boss Battle fights.
- Levels 9 and 10 take place in Phoenix as desert scavenger hunts. Level 9 has 2 pieces; Level 10 has 3 pieces, with the last treasure behind the boss.
- Levels 11 and 12 take place in Russia with caves and little cities in the background as regular fights.
- Levels 13 and 14 take place in China as regular fights where villains only shoot Made in China blocks or Chinese chicken pieces.
- Levels 15 and 16 take place in Canada as a treasure-map hunt. Clue guards like snow carrots and ice monsters must be beaten to earn map halves, then the boss guards the Canada star where the X marks the spot.
- Levels 17 and 18 take place in Africa Savanna. The heroes and playable villains keep their normal look, but their Big Bad Boss Battle 3 animal power names temporarily return for these two levels.
- Levels 19 and 20 take place inside the White House in Washington D.C. The mission is to protect President Trump from goofy hazards until the timer runs out. Level 19 lasts 2 minutes; Level 20 lasts 3 minutes. President Trump has 5 protection hearts and the player has 6 hearts.
- Levels 21 through 24 take place inside the Iran palace. They are regular fights with charge-ups around the board; walking over a charge-up collects it. Charge-ups can heal 4 or 5 hearts, give a 1-minute shield, or give a special power charge.
- Levels 25 and 26 take place in Israel by the wall. The hero can run through wall openings and come out the other side while the villain chases. Level 25 has a 2-minute timer; Level 26 has a 3-minute timer. The player wins by defeating the villain before time runs out, or by ending with more hearts than the villain.
- Levels 27 through 30 take place inside the Egyptian palace. They use the same charge-up idea as Iran, but with Egyptian palace visuals and stronger-feeling treasure-style rewards.
- The game uses the original Big Bad Boss Battle hero and villain look.
- Each level lets the player choose Mischievous Mayer, Yapping Yonatan, or the new Ice Boss.
- Ice Boss is blue, big, icy, and has an evil face with a Freeze Stomp attack.
- BBB4 uses regular superhero powers, not the BBB3 animal powers.
- Later levels get harder without adding boss hearts.
- More world surprises can be added next.
